④ Biology — Mendelian Genetics 🧬
Q4. In Mendelian Genetics, what is the expected genotypic ratio in a dihybrid cross in the F2 generation?
A
3:1
B
9:3:3:1 ✔
C
1:2:1
D
1:1:1:1
✅ Answer: B — Dihybrid cross F2 genotypic ratio = 9:3:3:1

Three Laws of Mendel (Father of Genetics):

  • Law of Dominance — Dominant trait expresses
  • Law of Segregation — Alleles separate into different gametes
  • Law of Independent Assortment — Genes inherited independently
MONOHYBRID CROSS
Single trait crossed
Phenotypic ratio: 3:1
Genotypic ratio: 1:2:1
DIHYBRID CROSS
Two traits crossed
Phenotypic ratio: 9:3:3:1
Genotypic ratio: 9:3:3:1

Q16. Which Indian city receives less rainfall during monsoon due to its location on leeward side?
A
Mumbai
B
Pune ✔
C
Cherrapunji
D
Goa
✅ Answer: B — Pune = Leeward side (Rain Shadow area) | Mumbai, Cherrapunji, Goa = Windward side
WINDWARD SIDE
Moisture-laden winds rise
Form clouds → Heavy rainfall
Ex: Mumbai, Cherrapunji, Goa, Mawsynram
LEEWARD SIDE (RAIN SHADOW)
Air descends → No rain
Ex: Pune, Interior Karnataka
Lonavala (Pune district) gets rain — on hill top

Monsoon Facts:

  • Word derived from Arabic "Mausam" = Season
  • Enters: First week of June (Kerala)
  • Covers all India by: Mid-July
  • Highest rainfall: Mawsynram (Meghalaya, Khasi Hills)
  • Retreating monsoon: Oct-Nov → Tamil Nadu gets rain

㉓ Economics — Law of Demand 📈
Q23. Which explains that for necessary goods, law of demand cannot operate?
A
Luntiff Paradox
B
Paradox of Thrift
C
Easterlin Paradox
D
Giffen Paradox ✔
✅ Answer: D — Giffen Paradox = For necessary goods, demand rises even when price rises
GIFFEN GOODS
Necessity goods (bread, rice)
Price ↑ → Demand ↑ (opposite of law of demand)
Reason: No money left for other goods
VEBLEN GOODS
Luxury goods (iPhone, designer items)
Price ↑ → Demand ↑
Reason: Social status symbol
Other Paradoxes
ParadoxMeaning
Paradox of ThriftIf everyone saves during recession, recession worsens (John Maynard Keynes)
Easterlin ParadoxHigher income ≠ Higher happiness

Law of Demand:

Price ↑ → Demand ↓ (Normal case) | Giffen & Veblen goods are exceptions
