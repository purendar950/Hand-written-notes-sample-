Five Writs — Complete Overview

Habeas Corpus"To have the body"Against unlawful detentionAnyone (including private individuals)
Mandamus"We command"To perform mandatory public dutyPublic authorities (NOT President/Governor | NOT voluntary duties)
Certiorari"To be certified"Higher court quashes lower court order (jurisdictional error)Lower courts/tribunals (NOT legislative bodies)
Prohibition"To prohibit"Prevent lower court from exceeding jurisdictionLower courts (NOT administrative/legislative authorities)

SC vs HC — WRIT JURISDICTION
SC (Art 32): FR only | Cannot refuse | Fundamental Right
HC (Art 226): FR + Legal Rights | Can refuse | Wider jurisdiction

Deccan Traps — Key Facts

Deccan Traps Formation:

  • Formed by Flood Basalt volcanism — most fluid lava type
  • Composed of Basaltic rock (Igneous, extrusive)
  • Forms Black soil / Regur soil — self-flowing, impermeable
  • Related to Reunion Hotspot — Indian plate moved over it
IGNEOUS ROCKS — TWO TYPES
Intrusive/Plutonic: Cools inside Earth → Granite
Extrusive/Volcanic: Cools on surface → Basalt
CRUST COMPOSITION
Continental crust: Made of Granite — Thick but less dense
Oceanic crust: Made of Basalt — Thin but denser

  • A = Afghanistan (NWFP)
