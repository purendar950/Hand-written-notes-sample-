⑫ Economy + Science — Copper & Metal Extraction 🔬
Q12. India has turned into a net importer of refined copper. Consider these statements: S1: Net importer of copper recently. S2: Rajasthan, MP, Jharkhand are the ONLY states involved in copper production. S3: During copper smelting, SO₂ and Particulate Matter are predominantly released.
A
Only 1 and 3 correct ✔
B
Only 2 and 3
C
All three
D
Only 1 and 2
✅ S1 ✔ true | S2 ✗ "only" = extreme keyword = LIKELY FALSE | S3 ✔ no extreme keyword = positive vibes

Extreme Keyword Trick: Statement 2 uses word "ONLY" — if ANY 4th state produces copper, statement is FALSE. Always be suspicious of "only", "always", "never", "solely" in exam statements!

Metal Extraction / Smelting Process
Step 1 Concentration of Ore — remove gangue particles (Hydraulic washing / Froth Flotation / Magnetic Separation / Leaching)
Step 2 Convert to Oxide: High reactivity → Electrolysis | Carbonate (medium) → Calcination (no air) | Sulfide (medium) → Roasting (with air)
Step 3 Reduction → Purification
Step 4 Electrolytic Refining — Anode = impure metal (oxidation) | Cathode = pure metal deposited (reduction) | Anode Mud = impurities | Electrolyte = salt of metal
FUN FACT
First metal discovered by humans = Copper

Both A and R true; R is correct explanation ✔
B

A and C are correct ✔
