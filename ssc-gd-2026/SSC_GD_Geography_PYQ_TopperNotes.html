① Demography, Minerals & Space 🌍
Q1. According to the Demographic Transition Theory, in which stage of population growth is India currently?
A
High stationary
B
Early expanding
C
Late expanding ✔
D
Low stationary
✅ Answer: C — Late expanding (the answer accepted by SSC). The Demographic Transition Model (DTM) tracks birth & death rates over stages.

India's 4 Phases of population growth:

  • Phase 1 (1901–1921): Stagnant / high stationary
  • Phase 2 (1921–1951): Early expanding — 1921 = "Year of the Great Divide"
  • Phase 3 (1951–1981): Late expanding (current per SSC)
  • Phase 4 (1981–present): Slowing growth — India now below replacement fertility

Q62. A lunar eclipse occurs when Earth blocks sunlight from reaching the Moon. If Earth were twice its current size, how would a lunar eclipse change?
A
The eclipse would last longer ✔
B
It would last shorter
C
No lunar eclipse would occur
D
No change
✅ Answer: A — A bigger Earth = bigger shadow → eclipse lasts longer. (Lunar eclipse = full moon; solar eclipse = new moon.)
SHADOWS
Umbra = dark (full) shadow · Penumbra = light (partial) shadow. "Blood Moon" = red Moon during a total lunar eclipse (red has the longest wavelength).
