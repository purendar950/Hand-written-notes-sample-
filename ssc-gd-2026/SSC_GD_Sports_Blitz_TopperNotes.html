💡 Topper Tips — Sports Ke Liye Yaad Rakho!
  • India has NOT won Asian Games gold in Weightlifting — but HAS in Shooting, Golf, Athletics & Football (2x)
  • Olympic flag = 5 rings (NOT 7!) — a common trap
  • India has hosted the Asian Games twice: 1951 & 1982 (both New Delhi)
  • India's LAST hockey Olympic gold = 1980 Moscow — captained by V. Bhaskaran
  • Women's World Cup (Cricket) came BEFORE Men's (1973 vs 1975) — reverse of common assumption!
  • 2023 Cricket World Cup = first-ever solely India-hosted edition (no co-hosts)
  • 2018 & 2022 CWG: Australia consistently #1 — India dropped from 3rd (2018) to 4th (2022)
