BRICS — Summit Timeline & Key Facts
2009 BRIC formed by Brazil, Russia, India, China — 1st Summit at Yekaterinburg, Russia
2010 South Africa joins → becomes "BRICS" — 2nd Summit held in Brazil
2011 3rd Summit — Sanya, China
2012 4th Summit — India (New Delhi)
2014 Fortaleza Agreement — created the New Development Bank & the Contingent Reserve Arrangement (CRA), corpus = $100 billion
2016 8th Summit — Goa, India
2021 13th Summit — New Delhi, India (virtual). India took over BRICS chairmanship from Russia this year
2024 BRICS → "BRICS Plus" — new members: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Iran, Indonesia, Egypt, Ethiopia
2025 17th Summit — Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
2026 18th Summit — New Delhi, India (upcoming)

BRICS — Institutions:

  • BRICS has NO permanent headquarters of its own (important trap!)
  • New Development Bank (NDB) — HQ: Shanghai, China
  • Contingent Reserve Arrangement (CRA) — created by the 2014 Fortaleza Agreement; corpus = $100 billion

Pongal — 4-Day Breakdown
Day 1 Bhogi Pongal — bonfires lit, Bhog offered at dawn, houses cleaned
Day 2 Thai Pongal (Surya Pongal) — Lord Surya worshipped; the "Pongal" dish (rice + milk boiled in an earthen pot) is prepared — "Pongal" literally means "to boil"
Day 3 Mattu Pongal — cattle/livestock worshipped, bathed, painted, adorned with bells
Day 4 Kanu Pongal (Kaanum Pongal) — families reunite, visit each other
🧠 Remember the order: Bhogi → Surya (Thai) → Mattu → Kanu

  • SAARC formed in Dhaka but HQ is Kathmandu, Nepal — formation city ≠ HQ city
  • "Baul" = West Bengal, but "Hurkiya Baul" = Uttarakhand — read the FULL term!
  • Godachi fair = Karnataka (NOT Gujarat — common trap)
  • 4 Natya Shastra instrument categories: Tat (string), Sushir (wind), Ghan (solid), Avanaddha (percussion)
  • Pongal order: Bhogi → Surya/Thai → Mattu → Kanu
  • "ONLY / ALL" extreme keywords in options = usually the FALSE/wrong choice
