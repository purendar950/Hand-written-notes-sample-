Dakshinayan = Sun moves Southward

Sun moves from Capricorn → Cancer

Tropic of Capricorn = 23½° South
Torrid Zone = Between both tropics = Direct rays

Magadha — Dynasty Sequence

ALEXANDER & BATTLE OF HYDASPES
Alexander attacked NW India during Dhanananda's reign
He fought Porus at Battle of Hydaspes (on banks of Jhelum River)
Hydaspes = ancient name of Jhelum River
Alexander won but returned Porus his territory
After Alexander: Seleucid Empire (founded by Seleucus Nicator, Alexander's general)
Chandragupta defeated Seleucus and married his daughter Helena
Seleucus sent Megasthenes → wrote Indica
AJATASHATRU — KEY FACTS
Son of Bimbisara
Called "Ajatashatru" = enemy of his clan
Reason: allegedly killed father Bimbisara (or forced him to commit suicide)
Capital shifted: Rajgriha → Pataliputra (by Udai, Ajatashatru's son)
