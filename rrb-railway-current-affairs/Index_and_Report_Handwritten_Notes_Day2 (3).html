PEACE — GLOBAL PEACE INDEX (GPI) 2026
Q1.
Which country ranked 1st in the Global Peace Index 2026?
✔ Answer: Iceland 🥇 (2nd New Zealand, 3rd Switzerland)
  • Released by: Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP), Sydney, Australia.
  • 2026 = 20th edition; 163 countries/territories evaluated (~99.7% of world population).
  • India rank = 127th.
  • Memory trick: Ice is cold → cold = cool = peace!
📌 Don't confuse with HDR (130), Gender Gap (131) & Economic Freedom Index (132) — all similar-looking India ranks!
PEACE — GLOBAL TERRORISM INDEX
Q2.
Which country topped the Global Terrorism Index?
✔ Answer: Pakistan 🥇 (2nd Burkina Faso, 3rd Niger)
  • Also released by IEP (same body as GPI).
  • India rank = 13th, score 6.428.
  • Niger: 3rd in terrorism but LAST in Global Innovation Index — funny contrast!

Q3.

HAPPINESS — WORLD HAPPINESS REPORT 2026
Q9.

Which state ranked 1st (Achiever category)?

DEFENCE — NEW CHIEF OF THE NAVAL STAFF
Q34.
Who was appointed as the new Chief of the Indian Navy, effective 31 May 2026?
✔ Answer: Admiral Krishna Swaminathan — Option A
OptionNameWhat they actually became
A ✔Krishna Swaminathan27th Chief of the Naval Staff
BDhiraj Seth31st Chief of the Army Staff
CN. S. Raja Subramani3rd Chief of Defence Staff (CDS)
DAjay Kochhar48th Vice Chief of the Naval Staff
✍️ Correction to my rough notes: Option D — Vice Admiral Ajay Kochhar took over as the 48th VCNS, not the 40th. Also the official spellings are Dhiraj Seth (not "Dheeraj") and Ajay Kochhar (not "Kochar") — easy to mishear when copying from a video/lecture.
Explanation:
  • 31 May 2026: Admiral Krishna Swaminathan took charge as the country's 27th Chief of the Naval Staff, succeeding Admiral Dinesh Kumar Tripathi. Tenure runs up to 31 Dec 2028.
  • 29 May 2026: Vice Admiral Ajay Kochhar took charge as the 48th Vice Chief of the Naval Staff, after serving as C-in-C, Andaman & Nicobar Command (India's only tri-service command).
  • 31 May 2026: General N. S. Raja Subramani became the country's 3rd Chief of Defence Staff (CDS), succeeding General Anil Chauhan — and also serves as Secretary, Department of Military Affairs.
  • 30 June 2026: General Dhiraj Seth took charge as the 31st Chief of the Army Staff, succeeding General Upendra Dwivedi. He is the first Armoured Corps officer to lead the Army since 1997.
