① Biology — Carbohydrates Classification 🧬
Q1. What are oligosaccharides?
A
Carbohydrates that yield one monosaccharide unit on hydrolysis
B
Carbohydrates that yield more than 10 monosaccharide units on hydrolysis
C

D
Carbohydrates that do not yield any monosaccharide units
✅ Answer: C — Oligosaccharides yield 2-10 monosaccharide units | 1 unit = Monosaccharide | >10 units = Polysaccharide
Carbohydrate Classification
TypeMonosaccharide UnitsExamples

Cereals/Grains → Maltose
Milk → Lactose (Lactose intolerance!)
