B
Bibi Ka Maqbara

D

SSC GD PYQ Q6. Why wasn't the Zabt (Zabti) system implemented in provinces like Gujarat and Bengal under the Mughal Empire?
A
Lack of trained officials
B
High rainfall
C
Difficulty in surveying land ✔
D
Opposition from farmers
✅ Answer: C — Surveying land in Gujarat & Bengal was very difficult
  • Zabti/Zabt = Dahsala system introduced in 1580 by Akbar's finance minister Todar Mal
  • Dahsala = 10-year average-based land revenue
  • Land divided into 4 categories: Polaj (never fallow), Parauti (1–2 years fallow), Chachar (3–4 years fallow), Banjar (5+ years fallow)
SSC GD PYQ Q7. In the Mansabdari system, what term referred to the cavalrymen maintained by the Mansabdar?
A
Zat
B
Sawar ✔
C
Mansab
D
Jagir
✅ Answer: B — Sawar = cavalrymen/horsemen
  • Zat = foot soldiers
  • Mansab = rank
  • Mansabdari ranged from 10 to 10,000
  • Higher mansabs reserved for Sultan's sons
  • Two types of payment: Naqdi (cash, for small mansabs) and Jagirdari (land grants, for large mansabs)
  • System introduced during Akbar's time

SSC GD PYQ Q15. The Iqta system under the Sultanate resembled the feudal system of Medieval Europe mainly because:

Revenue rights in exchange for military service ✔
B
Religious control of land
C
Democratic land distribution
D
Tax-free land grants
