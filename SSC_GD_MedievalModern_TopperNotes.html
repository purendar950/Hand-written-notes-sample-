BATTLES OF TARAIN

A
Lack of officials
B
High rainfall
C
Difficulty in surveying land ✔

✅ Answer: C — Surveying land was very difficult there
Zabt/Zabti (Dahsala) System — Key Points
ZABT SYSTEM
Zabt = Zabti = Dahsala System (1580, Akbar's time)
Implemented by: Raja Todar Mal
Method: 10-year average land revenue calculation
NOT applied in Gujarat & Bengal due to difficulty in land surveying
Land Categories under Zabt
CategoryMeaningFallow Period

BanjarLong-term fallow5+ years
